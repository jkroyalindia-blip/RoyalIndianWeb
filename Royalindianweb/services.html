For Resorts & Hotels

For Guest Houses & Service Apartments

For Restaurants & QSR Chains

Food Donation Initiative

At Royal Indian Kitchen, food is not just a service, it is a social responsibility. Through our dedicated food donation initiative, we prepare and distribute fresh, hygienic, and nutritious meals to those in need, funded and supported by Manasu Wellness & Research Center.

Our Commitment to Care

As part of our core mission, we allocate 25% of every rupee earned towards pediatric cancer support programs and free food distribution for underprivileged communities.

Who We Serve

  • Pediatric cancer patients and their families
  • Economically disadvantaged families
  • Migrant workers and daily wage earners
  • Patients, attendants and differently-abled individuals

Our Approach

We operate with a compassion-driven model, where meals are prepared with the same care as for our own families. Even beyond operational capacity, we continue to extend support because we believe no one should go hungry during times of need.

How to Support

  • Monetary contributions, even Rs. 1 makes a difference
  • Raw materials such as rice, cooking oil, pulses, vegetables and groceries
  • Volunteering through cooking, distribution and operational help
